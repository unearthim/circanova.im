Source Signal: September 2021

Taliban Consolidates Power in Afghanistan: The Taliban captured Panjshir Province, the final pocket of resistance, completing their takeover. They held a military parade showcasing captured US military equipment, solidifying their new rule.

Guinea's Government Overthrown in Military Coup: Special forces arrested President Alpha Condé, dissolved the government, and seized power, ending his controversial third term and imposing a new military order.

Germany's Election Marks End of Merkel Era: Federal elections to replace Angela Merkel after 16 years were too close to call, signaling a period of political uncertainty and a fractured power structure in Europe's largest economy.

Lebanon Forms Government After 13-Month Crisis: After more than a year of political paralysis and economic collapse following the Beirut blast, a new government was finally formed under billionaire Najib Mikati.

Ecuador Prison Riot Kills 116: A horrific prison riot attributed to gang warfare resulted in a massacre, prompting a state of emergency and highlighting the state's failure to maintain order.

Commentary: The Imposed Signal

The glitch for this era is this "Imposed Signal." It is the experience of watching a corrupted, noisy landscape being paved over by a stark, new geometry of power. This artifact simulates that process. The background is a field of chaotic data, the remnants of the old, failed signal. Over this, a clean, rigid grid is being drawn.

The ghost in this machine is the act of erasure. Your cursor is the focal point of this imposition, the lens of the world's attention.
